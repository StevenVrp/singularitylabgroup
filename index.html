About Singularity Lab

Singularity Lab is a service learning group dedicated to making physical science accessible and exciting for everyone. We believe that science should be engaging, visual, and relevant to everyday life.

Our mission is to spread physical science basics and spark people's interest in it through creating lectures, trivia, and videos posted on social media. We focus on exploring debated and visually appealing physical science topics that capture attention and inspire curiosity.

Whether you're a student preparing for exams, someone writing an essay, or just a curious mind, our resources are designed to help you understand complex concepts in simple, enjoyable ways.
