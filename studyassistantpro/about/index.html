About Study Assistant Pro

Study Assistant Pro is a lightweight, client-side productivity suite designed to help students manage their academic workload efficiently. It features a comprehensive homework task manager with progress analytics, a built-in Pomodoro timer, and even includes a fun Nim game for breaks!

The application is fully open-source and developed by StevenVRP. It's licensed under the MIT License, allowing free use, modification, and distribution.

Key features include:

  • Task Management: Organize tasks by subject, priority, and time slots.
  • Analytics: Visualize your productivity with interactive charts.
  • Pomodoro Timer: Stay focused with a customizable study timer.
  • Voice Control: Manage tasks hands-free using voice commands.
  • Reminders: Never miss a deadline with smart notifications.
  • Nim Game: Take a quick break with the classic strategy game.

The entire project is built with modern web technologies: HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript. It runs entirely in your browser—no server required! Your data stays private on your device via localStorage.
